About

Development

This website is intended to be used for a Pizza restaurant looking to expand their reach beyond their physical location.
Steps that were taken to create this site started with a clear list on what was needed to be done before completing a wireframe and then coding.

Design Choices

Aesthetic choices were made to follow the subject of the page. The color palette choices for this website will all be around colors that are found on a pizza. Primary colors like red and green will be complemented with browns and beiges. It’s a limited palette that will work well in being appeasing for the eye. Typography was chosen based on legibility and how appealing and eye catching.

Improvement

I would like to work on the functionality of databases for accounts as well as shopping carts.
